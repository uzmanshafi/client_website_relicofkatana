The Relic of Katana

The relic of Katana is an ancient, cursed, deadly, blood stained sword used by the greatest Samurai Warlord to ever have lived. His name, Ojin. It has taken the lives of thousands of enemies and still continues to do so to this very day. Prophecy has it that the soul of Ojin now lives within the sword, and all those who are unworthy and touch the sword, and falsely claim rightful ownership, will be struck down by lightening and sent to a world of eternal darkness.
